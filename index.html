Who we are

ProFirm Limited is a Professional Services Firm of Professional Service Partners with expertise in varying areas of the service industry.


We are an Anchor Firm that specializes in providing Project Management Consulting and Service Supply Chain Management Business Process Outsourcing (SSCMBPO) Solutions that drives growth and transformation.

What we do

We leverage our Service Supply Chain Management Partners (SSCMP) to offer a comprehensive portfolio of Professional Services Outsourcing solutions that gives companies the opportunity to outsource non-core project, task-based, knowledge-intensive and industry-specific business processes that can be better delivered by a trusted outsourced partner.


Our Service Supply Chain Management covers seven key areas including; Professional Services Outsourcing, Project Management Consulting, Marketing, Information Technology (I.T), Creative, Administration and Education/Training.

How we do it

1.
We engage our clients at a strategic level to design and customize high-level business process solutions.


2.
Our team of experts translate and integrate our Clients high-level Business Processes seamlessly into our Service Supply Chain Management Value Network to create a more detailed project plan that forms the basis of the implementation process.


3.
We source and Collaborate with our Service Supply Chain Management Partners to implement the solutions we designed in an effort to deliver customer satisfaction cost-effectively.
